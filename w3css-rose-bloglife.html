EDUC TOUR 2025

DAY 1

FULLY BOOKED

2025 / 04 / 07

In my first day in manila, i've got to visit in my favorite book store, the " FULLY BOOKED " 'cause i loved anime, and different kind of manga book is there.

DAY 2

SUBIC EXPO

2025-04-08

The Subic Expo was a vibrant showcase of local culture, products, and innovation, featuring diverse exhibits and live performances. It offered an engaging experience for visitors, blending entertainment with opportunities to support regional businesses.

DAY 3

NATIONAL MUSEUM

2025-04-09

The National Museum offered a rich cultural experience, showcasing the Philippines' heritage through art, archaeology, and natural history exhibits. Walking through its halls provided a deeper appreciation of the country's vibrant history and artistic legacy.

It amazed me to see the history of the Philippines and all the treasures that they have gathered. And the bones of animals, it was amazing that they have preserved it for such a long time.

DAY 4

MUSEO NG PAMPANGULONG SASAKYAN

2025-04-10

The MUSEUM OF PAMPANGULONG SASAKYAN, Ang Museo ng Pampangulong Sasakyan sa Quezon Memorial Circle ay isang makasaysayang destinasyon na nagpapakita ng koleksyon ng mga sasakyang ginamit ng mga dating Pangulo ng Pilipinas. Ang bawat sasakyan ay may kasamang impormasyon tungkol sa kasaysayan nito at sa Pangulong gumamit nito, na nagbibigay ng mas malalim na pag-unawa sa kasaysayan ng bansa.

DAY 5

METROPOLITAN MANILA DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY

2025-04-11

The MMDA offered insightful exposure to how urban planning, traffic management, and disaster response are handled in Metro Manila. It was both educational and eye-opening, highlighting the agency’s vital role in maintaining order and safety in a complex metropolitan area.

DAY 6

HYTEC POWER INC.

2025-04-12

The Hytec Power Inc. provided a hands-on look into advanced training equipment and innovative technology used in technical education. It was an inspiring visit that emphasized the importance of industry-based learning for future-ready skills.

DAY 7

LRT STATION

2025-04-13

The LRT station visit offered a behind-the-scenes look at the operations and management of urban rail transit in Metro Manila. It highlighted the importance of efficient public transportation in easing traffic congestion and supporting daily commuters.

DAY 8

BAGUIO CITY

2025-04-14

The Baguio City visit was a refreshing experience, with its cool climate, scenic views, and rich cultural attractions. Exploring landmarks like Burnham Park, Mines View, and the Baguio Public Market made the trip both relaxing and memorable.
